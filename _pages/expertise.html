Of course you want a web developer with great technical skills. That’s the easy part.

But you also need someone who can design and write in a way that appeals to your audience. Someone who has the training skills to teach you how to manage your website. Someone who can give you great support if anything goes wrong. And, if possible, someone who really understands the sector you work in and how it works.

Read on to find out more about my skills, both technical and otherwise, and the sectors I understand best.
