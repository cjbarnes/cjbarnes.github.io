My projects begin with a simple idea:

You don’t want a website.

What you do want is better marketing, more students, more sales, easier communication, or better information management. Your website is just a means to an end.

So the key to making a great website—that’s also great value for money—is understanding what the site’s purpose is. That’s our job, working together. Then it’s up to me to create a website precisely focused around that purpose.
